Nostalgia

Conclusion

Use nostalgia responsibly. Enjoy the style of the past, but live in the present. Use good memories to motivate you to make new ones. Enjoy the good that the past has to offer, but also be wary of the not so good times that were left behind. Know what parts of the past should stay in the past. Try to find peace in what went wrong in the past, and get closure, if possible. No matter what happens, stay grounded in the present, and keep moving forward.
